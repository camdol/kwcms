Bold & sleek The Kia EV5
Design
Bold & sleek all-electric SUV
Inspired by Kia's design pillar 'Bold for Nature', EV5 is designed to give a life-enriching experience that goes beyond the physical boundaries of the car itself.
Design
Blend of opposing elements
Inspired by the contrast of man-made sitting in nature, the interior of EV5 brings unique balance of boldness with softness.
Advanced Technology
HDA2 (Highway Driving Assist 2)
EV5 will be equipped with HDA2, which helps maintain distance from the vehicle ahead, maintain the set speed, and keep the vehicle centered in the lane while driving on highways.
Advanced Technology
RSPA2 (Remote Smart Parking Assist 2)
Parking convenience has been enhanced with RSPA2, a highly accurate and convenient parking assistance function that recognises parking lines on its own.
Advanced Technology
V2G (Vehicle-to-grid)
EV5 features V2G capability, a cutting-edge technology that enables EV5 to supply charged electricity to the power grid even when the vehicle is not running.
Advanced Technology
Connectivity
With an optimised ccNC(connected car navigation cockpit), EV5 features an advanced infotainment system that intuitively delivers information to the driver.
Battery & PE System
Battery system
EV5's battery is engineered to function effectively even in extreme hot and cold weather through an advanced heat pump system that manages the battery’s temperature, ensuring consistent performance regardless of external conditions.
Battery & PE System
PE system
EV5's dual-motor all-wheel-drive setup, featuring a 160kW motor in the front and a 70kW motor at the rear, ensures optimal performance thanks to an improved PE system.
