Recall No.
202118999
Recall Date
8 Mar 2022
Campaign
Fuel pump
Affected Models
Model year 2021 Carnival
Model year 2016-2022 K5
Description
The fuel supply pipe may have been damaged during manufacturing, which may result in a fuel leak.
Risk
If a fuel leak occurs, this can result in an engine compartment fire. A vehicle fire could increase the risk of an accident, serious injury or death to vehicle occupants and other road users.
Counter Measure
All affected vehicles will have the fuel line checked replaced if required.
