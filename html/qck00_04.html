Controlling the Regenerative Braking Level

How to adjust the regenerative braking level

The intensity of regenerative braking on Kia's EV models can be adjusted from 0 to 3 levels using the paddle shifter on the steering wheel.
  1. 01 Access the infotainment display in the vehicle and go to [EV] > [EV settings] > [Winter mode].
  2. 02Check the box next to the winter mode option to activate it.
  3. 03A snowflake symbol will appear on the battery gauge on the instrument panel during charging when the winter mode is activated.
  4. 04To cancel the winter mode, simply uncheck the box in the same manner as above.

Instead of tapping with your hands, can also use the heat from your hands or something warm, like a hand warmer, to remove the ice.

  1. 05Access the infotainment display in the vehicle and go to [EV] > [EV settings] > [Winter mode].

Cautions

  • Dirt, snow, or foreign substances on the front radar sensor lens or its cover may affect its sensing performance. If this happens, the system may not work correctly.
  • If the warning light for the Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist (FCA) is on, the smart regeneration system may not work correctly.
  • The smart regeneration system is designed to provide driving convenience, but the driver must exercise caution and make driving decisions based on their own discretion to ensure safe driving.

The availability and operation of features may vary depending on the model, trim, and region. For detailed information, please refer to the owner’s manual for your vehicle.
